How much does Alabama car shipping cost?

Many factors go into calculating the cost of your Alabama car shipment:

  • Total travel distance
  • Pickup and delivery locations
  • Shipping dates
  • Trailer type (open or enclosed)
  • Vehicle details

For the most accurate estimate, provide a Montway agent with your vehicle type, origin and destination locations, first available shipping date, and specific trailer type (open or enclosed).

How long does it take to ship a car to or from Alabama?

The actual transit time of your vehicle depends on:

  • Total travel distance
  • Weather
  • Seasonal demand
  • Pickup & delivery location
  • Route construction
  • Route traffic

Transit time does not include vehicle loading and unloading time, or any scheduled stops the driver may have while en route. Once your order is dispatched, you’ll receive estimated pickup and delivery dates.

How much does it cost to ship a car to another state?

The cost to ship a car can vary depending on the distance between the pickup and delivery locations, the type of vehicle being shipped, and the desired timeframe for delivery. On average, the cost can be anywhere from $600 to $1,000 or more. It's important to keep in mind that additional fees may apply for things like expedited shipping or enclosed transport, so it's always a good idea to get detailed quotes before making any decision.

Distance

Total distance is the main factor in determining the cost of car shipping. A distance of less than 600 miles is around $0.89/mile, between 600 and 1,600 miles about $0.78/mile, and distances more than 1,500 miles are roughly $0.58/mile.

Route

The route refers to what highways or roads the car carrier will need to travel to transport your vehicle. Congested city or rural mountain roads may affect costs.

Vehicle Year/Make/Model

Cars come in different sizes—an economy car will be lighter and use less space, while larger or modified cars may require adjustments, increasing costs.

Date

It's best to book your auto transport service at least two weeks in advance, as availability and demand can impact costs.

Method of Transport

Open and enclosed transport options are available. Enclosed transport costs more but offers additional protection.

Condition of Vehicle

Knowing the condition of the vehicle being transported is critical, as inoperable vehicles require special equipment, increasing costs.

Choosing the best state-to-state auto transport company

Your vehicle is very important to you and you’ll want to work with a trustworthy auto transport company. When starting your research, look for a company with years of experience, a good reputation, and positive reviews from previous customers. Always confirm that the company you are considering is insured and licensed as well. Overall, they should work with you to understand your budget and timeframe and recommend the best auto transport option possible.
